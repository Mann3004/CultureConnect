About Sabarmati Ashram

Sabarmati Ashram is located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. It was the residence of Mahatma Gandhi and served as the epicenter of the Indian freedom movement.

History

The ashram was established in 1917 and was home to Gandhi and his followers for over 12 years. It played a crucial role in the Indian independence movement.
